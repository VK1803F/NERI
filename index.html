Ask Us Anything →
Is CycleWell a hospital or clinic?
+

No. CycleWell is a wellness and consultancy platform — not a clinical establishment. We do not diagnose, prescribe medication, or perform clinical procedures. We provide lifestyle support, nutrition guidance, fitness coaching, mental wellness, and referrals to empanelled medical professionals.

Can CycleWell replace my gynaecologist?
+

Absolutely not — and we never claim to. We strongly encourage all members to have a qualified gynaecologist overseeing their medical care. CycleWell complements clinical care by providing the lifestyle and emotional support that most doctors simply don't have time to provide during a 10-minute appointment.

Is my health data safe?
+

Yes. We are fully compliant with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDPA). Health data is classified as sensitive personal data and we collect it only with your explicit consent. We never share your personal data with third parties without your permission.

I'm not from Chennai — can I still join?
+

Yes! CycleWell is a digital-first platform. While headquartered in Chennai and particularly focused on Tamil Nadu, our app, nutrition coaching, fitness programs, and mental wellness sessions are available to women anywhere in India.

What if I need to see a doctor urgently?
+

In any medical emergency, please call 112 or visit your nearest hospital immediately. CycleWell is not an emergency medical service. Our Premium plan includes doctor referral coordination for non-emergency situations.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
+

Yes. There are no lock-in contracts. You can cancel your monthly plan at any time from your account settings. Annual plans are non-refundable after 30 days but you retain full access for the entire paid period.
